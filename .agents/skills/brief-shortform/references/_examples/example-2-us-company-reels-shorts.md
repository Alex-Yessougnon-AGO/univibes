Reference brief for a US faceless consumer e-commerce brand. Company-mode, market=EN, hero=Reels + variant=Shorts.

This is a **worked example**. Actual hero brief artifact: `docs/forsvn/artifacts/marketing/brief-shortform/morning-light-hydrator/brief.md`.

## Setup

- **Angle:** "The minute that ruins the rest of your skin's day"
- **Brand mode:** company (faceless brand, e-com)
- **Market:** EN (US)
- **Production mode:** motion-graphic (company default)
- **Platforms:** Reels (hero) + Shorts (variant)
- **Polish chain:** none (EN company mode)

## Research signals

- Reels US e-com niche: pre-reveal tease + before/after dominate (combined 9/14 sample)
- Reels: Originality Score (platform leadership's Jan 2025) — original kinetic-type favored over reused audio
- Shorts: loop rate weighted; 3-second drop-off less severe than Reels (sample n=10, OK)
- Trending audio on Reels for skincare niche: original beat-bed common in top performers
- Sample-size flag: Reels OK (n=11), Shorts OK (n=10)
- BRAND.md voice: "warm, scientific-but-friendly, no sales-pitch"
- VoC from ICP: "I keep buying products that don't work on me", "My skin feels tight by 10am"

- **Style:** Vector flat + kinetic type, 3D product render

## Variant — Shorts (24s, 9:16)

### What Changed From Hero

| Element | Hero Reels | Variant Shorts | Why |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hook archetype | Pre-reveal tease | Demo-first (product on screen at 0:00) | Shorts swipe-decision in 1-2s; faster reveal |
| Audio | Original beat-bed | Silent-friendly + cleaner beat | Shorts has stronger silent-watch behavior; loop weighted |
| Caption | 380 chars, paragraph break | Title-as-hook (≤100 chars) + short desc | Shorts title carries discovery |
| CTA placement | End-card + caption | End-card + pinned comment | Shorts standard pattern |
| Length | 38s | 24s | Shorts sweet spot 15-60s; loop-friendly preferred shorter |
| Loop ending | None | Bottle returns to opening composition | Shorts loop rate signal |
| Pacing | Slow-build first 4s | Faster establishment, payoff at 0:08 | Shorts swipe-resistant earlier |

### Hook (0:00–0:02)
- **Visual:** Product bottle in opening frame, low-angle, brand color backdrop (matches final frame for loop)
- **Text overlay:** "30 sec to ruin your skin's day"
- **Audio:** Beat-bed entry; sound effect on bottle reveal
- **Archetype:** Demo-first (Shorts niche)
- **VoC anchor:** Same as hero ("My skin feels tight by 10am")

### Loop ending
- **0:22-0:24:** bottle returns to opening composition; brand-color backdrop matches 0:00
- Final frame is identical (or near-identical) to opening frame — clean loop back

### Caption / Title

**Title (≤100 chars):** "The 30 seconds that decide your skin's whole day. ↓"

**Description (excerpt):** "Why your face feels tight by 10am — and the 5% niacinamide + ceramide answer. morninglight.co"

### CTA
- **Type:** Subscribe-prompt + link in pinned comment
- **Placement:** End-card 0:22-0:24
- **Copy:** "Subscribe for skin science minus the hype" + pinned comment carries link

### Success Criteria
- Completion ≥30% (Shorts threshold)
- Loop rate ≥1.5 per view (sample observation)
- Shares meaningful uplift over baseline

---

## Why this works

- Hero on Reels bets on pre-reveal tease + before/after — both top archetypes per research
- Audio strategy is original (Originality Score) — kinetic type carries the hook, not voice or trending track
- Variant for Shorts truly recuts: demo-first hook (faster), silent-friendly beat, loop-friendly ending, title-as-hook
- Same VoC phrase across both platforms ("My skin feels tight by 10am") — voice consistency
- BRAND.md voice maintained: warm, scientific-but-friendly, no sales-pitch (no "buy now" CTAs)
- Success criteria platform-specific: Reels 60% 3s hold, Shorts 30% completion + loop rate
