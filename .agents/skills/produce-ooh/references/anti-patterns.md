# Anti-Patterns — produce-ooh

7 patterns. The critic checks each before ship.

1. **Two messages.** Trying to say two things on one placement. *Detect:* more than one idea/offer/claim. *Fix:* one idea; cut the rest (two messages = zero messages).

2. **Too many words.** Copy that can't be read in the dwell window. *Detect:* >7 words on a highway board (more is OK only for dwell placements). *Fix:* cut to the line ceiling for the placement.

3. **Low contrast.** Type that vanishes at distance or in sunlight/backlight. *Detect:* low foreground/background contrast; thin type. *Fix:* high-contrast pairing (Leaf/Cream on Forest Shadow); bold weight.

4. **Small print.** Legal/detail copy treated as readable. *Detect:* any copy that requires stopping to read. *Fix:* OOH carries no small print; route detail to a destination (URL/code), not the board.

5. **QR-as-CTA on a fast placement.** A QR code on a highway board nobody can scan at speed. *Detect:* QR is the primary CTA on a moving/fast placement. *Fix:* a memorable short URL/code for fast placements; QR only where viewers dwell (transit interior, station).

6. **Wrong dimensions/spec.** Designing to a guessed size/bleed/DPI. *Detect:* dimensions not traced to the vendor template. *Fix:* pull the vendor's template as the source of truth before specing.

7. **Off-brand.** Generic stock look; gradients/glass; accent overused. *Detect:* doesn't read as the brand at a glance. *Fix:* brand system, matte, Leaf <10%.
