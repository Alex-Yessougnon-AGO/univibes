# Worked Example — 45-min product-launch webinar

**Brief:** Launch webinar, 45 min, live stream. Audience: trial users + waitlist. Goal: convert to paid (the offer segment is protected). Cast: founder (host), a customer (story), a teammate (drives the demo tech).

## Run of Show (durations sum to 45:00, ~2 min buffer in Q&A)

| Start | Dur | Segment | Owner | Content beats | Cue (verbal · tech · trigger) | Fallback |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0:00 | 3:00 | Welcome + why-stay | Founder | hook, what they'll leave with, "stay for the offer" | "Welcome in" · lower-third up · producer | thin crowd → start anyway, recording rolls |
| 0:03 | 5:00 | The problem | Founder | the pain, sized; one stat | "You know the feeling…" · slide 2 · founder | — |
| 0:08 | 12:00 | Live demo | Teammate (drive) + Founder (narrate) | the ONE core action, end to end | "Let me show you" · cut to screen-share · teammate | **demo breaks → cut to pre-recorded demo clip** |
| 0:20 | 6:00 | Customer story | Customer | before/after, the number | "Meet [name]" · spotlight customer · producer | **no-show → founder reads the prepared quote + case** |
| 0:26 | 5:00 | The offer | Founder | the paid plan, the launch incentive, the link | "Here's the offer" · slide + link in chat · founder | — |
| 0:31 | 12:00 | Q&A | Founder | answer live; (buffer lives here) | "Drop questions" · pin chat · producer | **silent → host asks 3 seeded Qs** |
| 0:43 | 2:00 | Close + CTA | Founder | recap the offer, deadline, link again | "Last thing" · final slide · founder | overrun → cut Q&A to 10:00, protect this |

## Pre-Show Checklist
Mics tested · demo account loaded + the pre-recorded demo clip queued · slides cued · link pasted in chat draft · backup local recording rolling · customer mic'd + on standby · teammate has screen-share permission.

## Contingency Playbook
- **Demo breaks** → teammate plays the pre-recorded clip; founder narrates as if live; never debug on stream.
- **Customer no-show** → founder reads the prepared quote + walks the case slide (6:00 covered).
- **Stream fails** → "back in 60s" slide; restart; the recording catches up.
- **Silent Q&A** → 3 seeded questions ("How does this compare to X?", "What about Y?", "Pricing for teams?").
- **Overrun** → compress Q&A to 10:00 first; the offer + close are never cut.

## Critic → 43/50 PASS
Timing 9 (sums, buffer in Q&A), Ownership 9 (speaker + tech-driver split on the demo), Cue clarity 9, Goal service 8 (every segment serves convert-to-paid), Contingency 8 (all five SPOFs covered). PASS.
